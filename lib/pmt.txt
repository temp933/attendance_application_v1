**WHAT NEEDS TO BE ADDED:**

**Concept:** A leave policy can have two independent accrual engines running simultaneously, both feeding into the same employee leave balance.

**Engine 1 — Time Based Accrual (toggleable)**
- Admin enables/disables per policy
- Type: `yearly` (credit all at once on Jan 1 or employee joining date) or `monthly` (credit every 1st of month)
- Days: how many days to credit per period
- Example: 2 days every month, or 12 days every January 1st

**Engine 2 — Attendance Based Accrual (toggleable)**
- Admin enables/disables per policy
- Streak days: how many consecutive present days (excluding weekends and holidays per tenant's attendance policy) triggers a credit
- Reward days: how many days to credit when streak is completed
- Streak resets to 0 if employee is absent or takes leave on a working day
- Example: every 20 consecutive present days → earn 1 day

**Both engines can be ON simultaneously for the same policy.**
Example: Monthly 2 days + attendance bonus 1 day per 20 streak = same leave balance gets both credits.

**Carry Forward (already partially exists, keep and extend):**
- enabled/disabled
- reset type: yearly or monthly
- max carry forward days (optional cap, null = unlimited)

**Max Days:**
- Represents the annual cap on total balance (allocated + carried forward combined)
- Employee cannot exceed this cap regardless of accrual

```sql
CREATE TABLE leave_accrual_log (
  id            INT AUTO_INCREMENT PRIMARY KEY,
  tenant_id     VARCHAR(100) NOT NULL,
  emp_id        INT NOT NULL,
  leave_type_id INT NOT NULL,

```
The UNIQUE KEY on `reference` prevents double-crediting.
